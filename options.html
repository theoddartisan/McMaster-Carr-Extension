Enter custom naming conventions in the text box below.
- You can set substitutions for other words or characters here. You can set things like abbreviations to suit your style
- You enter the text you want to replace followed by an equal sign (=) followed by the text you want to use instead
- Separate each substitution with a semicolon (;)
- To remove text without substitution leave the right side of the equal sign (=) blank
- The left side of the equal sign (=) is not case sensitive but the right side is case sensitive
- These characters are not allowed in file names: / \ : * ? " < > | Do not include them in your substitutions
- Do not include spaces before or after any equal sign (=) or semicolon (;) unless specifically needed for creating your naming structure
